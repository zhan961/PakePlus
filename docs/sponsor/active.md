# Rewards

PakePlus is an open-source project on GitHub. Most features are free to use, but development, maintenance, and bug fixing require a significant amount of time and effort. Thank you for your support. As a token of appreciation, after sponsoring, you can contact us via email or WeChat to unlock additional privileges, including:

-   Private projects
-   Increased project limits
-   Removal of time restrictions
-   iOS signing-free packaging
-   Shareable downloads
-   Support for Windows 7 and portable (no-install) versions
-   Support for configuring app splash screens, and more

If you have contributed to any PakePlus repository, or have promoted PakePlus through videos or articles online, feel free to contact us. Based on your contributions and promotion efforts, we will grant you additional privileges. Thank you for your recognition and support of PakePlus.

---

## Activation Code

After receiving your activation code, go to the top-right corner of the PakePlus homepage, click on your avatar, and enter the **My Profile** page:

![](../../static/imgs/active1.webp)

Then click the **Upgrade** button and enter your activation code:

![](../../static/imgs/active3.webp)

For support on Windows 7, XP, and Linux ARM systems, you will need to manually agree before using the feature:

![](../../static/imgs/active4.webp)

**Private Projects**: The packaged content will be stored in your private GitHub repository, accessible only by you.

**Windows 7 & XP Support**: Supports packaging with Electron, including compatibility with Windows 7 and domestic Linux ARM systems. Simply select the Electron version when publishing:

![](../../static/imgs/electron2.webp)

---

## Notes

-   iOS signing-free packaging only supports online websites.
-   It does **not** support static files, advanced app configurations, splash screen configuration, or script injection.
-   In the Electron version, Tauri and PakePlus APIs are not supported.
-   Some advanced configuration options may not take effect in the Electron version.
